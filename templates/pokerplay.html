Play Poker

Practice your poker skills. Play heads-up poker with your phone or computer. Develop the skills of when to bet or when to fold, without the risk of losing real money, while having fun.

This game is between you, the player, and your computer or phone, the dealer. The pre-flop bet is $20. On the flop, you, the player, have to decide to proceed to the turn with a $30 bet, or fold, losing only the initial $20 bet. On the turn, you have to bet $50 to continue to the river, or fold, losing $50, which was the bet on pre-flop $20 and flop $30. On the river, the bet is $100 to proceed to the showdown, or fold, losing $100. At showdown, the player either wins or loses the total pot of $400.

In case you encounter an error in a page, click refresh to get back to the game.
